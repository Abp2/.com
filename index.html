DOON INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL

Doon International School was created as a journey forward in our aim to ignite the imagination of children. The continuing goal is to create fun and rewarding learning experiences that will last children with the rest of their learning lives.
Our aim is to create a purpose for learning and allow children to be thinkers, innovators and problem solvers; to orchestrate experiences that will result in a natural and integrated development of knowledge, skills and thinking.
Doon International School is an institution where education is disseminated with a core objective to create Culturally Developed, Responsible and Conscientious Global Citizens.
Education in such an ambience is a rewarding experience. Our main task is to kindle and empower the thought process of our students in a manner that they are able to cull out the essence and become the embodiment of wisdom.

ADMISSION QUERIES

REGISTRATION AND ADMISSION RULES:

1. The school Academic Year is from April to March. Student seeking fresh admission are normally admitted in April/ May and July August. However admission on transfer certificate are open throughout the year .

2. Names are registered up to the 15th of March of the year of the admission. Registration entitles the child to appear for the School Entrance Test which is held in the first week of April.

3. The registration fee is not refundable, regardless of whether the child sits for the entrance test or not or he/she is admitted or not.

4. All students are interviewed before they are granted admission.
